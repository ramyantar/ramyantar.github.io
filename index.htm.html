Short Background

I received my bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering from Assiut University - Egypt with Grade Excellent with honor and ranked the first out of 135 students. Then I worked as a Teaching and Research Assistant for 1 year and 6 months in Assiut University, Egypt. I am now a Master's Student inMarine & Maritime Intelligent Robotics (Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Degree) at Universite de Toulon in France and Universidad Jaume I in Spain , as a holder a scholarship from European Commission and one of 22 students who got selected to get this scholarship among 1000 applications. My interests currently are Robotic manipulation, Computer Vision, Machine Learning/AI, and Autonomous Systems. I hope to continue on to a Ph.D. related to these areas.
