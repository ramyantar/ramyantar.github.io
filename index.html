About

My name is Ramy Alham and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Marine & Maritime Intelligent Robotics (Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Degree) at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology,Norway and the University of Toulon, France. I am honored to have been selected as one of the 22 students to receive a scholarship from the European Commission out of 1000 applicants. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering (Design and Manufacturing) from Assiut University in Egypt, where I ranked first out of 135 students and received an Excellent with honor grade. After completing my degree, I worked as a Teaching and Research Assistant at Assiut University for over a year and a half, where I taught various mechanical design courses and conducted research in rehabilitation robotics.

I am a Certified SOLIDWORKS Expert in Mechanical Design and a Certified SOLIDWORKS Professional in Sheet Metal, Weldments, Surfacing, and Drawing Tools. As a mechanical design engineer, I have three years of experience designing and manufacturing machines and robotics. Through my work, I have gained extensive experience in designing and building robotic systems and have discovered a passion for learning about other robotics topics. As a marine robotics master's student, I gained a strong understanding of the principles of robotics, including modeling, control, ROS, autonomous systems, machine learning, computer vision, artificial intelligence, and marine sciencee. My interests currently are Robotic manipulation, Localization and Mapping, and Autonomous Systems. I hope to continue on to a Ph.D. related to these areas.
